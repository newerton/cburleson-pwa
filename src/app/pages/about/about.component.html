About

Hi, I'm Cody Burleson and this is my personal home base on the web. I use this site to do mad science - studying and experimenting with web development and various software tools. I often record my discoveries and lessons-learned here - mainly because I don't trust my own memory, but also in case it could be helpful to others. Sometimes, I also record thoughts about life in general.

I practice solution design and full-stack development for Base22. There, I also work on our flagship product, Carbon LDP - a linked data platform.

My hobbies are digital sculpting in ZBrush and writing. I'm 47 years old. I'm very happily married. I have two grown children and a dog.

Thanks for stopping by. I'm glad you're here.
